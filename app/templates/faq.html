Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

“Generate Plan” is disabled — how do I enable it?

Choose a major from the dropdown first. The button activates only after a major is selected. If the list is empty, refresh the page. Still empty? Try closing and reopening the site.

I clicked “Generate Plan” but it failed — what should I do?

1) Wait a few seconds and try again (the generator can be busy).
2) Make sure you have an internet connection.
3) If it still fails, return to the home page and re-select your major, then generate again.
4) If the issue repeats, take a screenshot and contact support with the time and what you selected.

The major list is empty — how can I fix this?

Try refreshing the page. If you still don’t see majors, please close the tab and reopen the planner. If it persists, contact support — the catalog may be updating.

I see “Missing prerequisites” — how do I resolve it?

Move the required unit(s) to an earlier semester. You can hover over a highlighted unit to see what it needs, or check the Validation panel for a bullet list of missing prerequisites. After rearranging, click “Validate Plan” to re-check.

A unit says it’s only available in a certain semester — what now?

Some units run only in Semester 1 or only in Semester 2. Drag the unit into a matching semester (e.g., move “Semester 1 only” units into Year 1/3/5). Then re-validate.

I can’t drag a unit or the semester won’t accept it — how can I fix this?

Each semester allows a maximum of 4 units. If a semester is full, remove a unit first or move it elsewhere. You can also drag a unit back to “Available Units” to free space. If dragging feels unresponsive, try a quick page refresh.

The Validation panel shows errors — what’s the best way to fix them?

Read the list from top to bottom and fix issues one at a time:
• If total units < 24, add more from “Available Units”.
• If a semester has > 4 units, move extras to another semester.
• For missing prerequisites, move required units earlier.
• Re-click “Validate Plan” after each change to confirm you’ve resolved it.

The PDF didn’t download — what should I try?

Make sure your browser allows downloads/pop-ups for this site. Try again, or switch to Chrome/Edge. If the issue continues, try exporting after a page refresh.

“AI Validate Plan” feels slow — is that normal?

Yes — the AI performs a deeper analysis and can take a little longer. You can keep editing your plan and use the standard “Validate Plan” for quick checks, then run the AI check when you’re ready.

How do I reset and start over?

Refresh the page to clear the board. Then pick your major again and click “Generate Plan”. You can always re-add units from the “Available Units” panel.

I think a unit’s information is wrong — how do I report it?

Take a screenshot and note the unit code, the semester, and what looks incorrect. Send it to the support contact listed on the home page so we can correct the data quickly.
